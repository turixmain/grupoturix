Biography

Alma Alejandra Luna Gómez is a specialist in Electronic Engineering and Telecommunications. She holds a Master's degree in Information and Communication Technologies and a Ph.D. in Education. Currently, she is pursuing a Ph.D. in Materials Science at the Polytechnic University of the Valley of Mexico (UPVM).

She serves as an Adjunct Professor at the National Autonomous University of Mexico (UNAM), specifically in the Engineering Department at FES Cuautitlán. Her academic background combines strong technical expertise in microwave links and computing systems with innovative educational strategies.

Her current research focuses on the development of advanced photonic materials, aiming to revolutionize optical systems for future applications in quantum communications.

Research Vision

"I propose advanced photonic materials that increase capacity, efficiency, and security in optical systems, paving the way for quantum communications."
