Welcome to Turix-Dynamics

Turix-Dynamics is a dedicated research group specializing in the analysis of dynamic systems, with a strong focus on fault diagnosis, detection, and fault-tolerant control. The group excels in robust control and integrates machine learning with mathematical and data-driven methodologies.

Their expertise includes working with Takagi-Sugeno systems, Linear Parameter Varying systems, and nonlinear equations. In recent years, research has expanded into data-driven diagnostic techniques, leveraging Artificial Neural Networks and Principal Component Analysis.

Leading innovation in mobile robotics, electromobility, digital twins, and autonomous systems.
