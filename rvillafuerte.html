Biography

Raúl Villafuerte Segura received his Doctor of Science degree in Automatic Control from the Center for Research and Advanced Studies (CINVESTAV-Zacatenco), Mexico City, in 2010.

He is currently a Full-Time Professor at the Academic Area of Computing and Electronics (AACyE) of the Institute of Basic Sciences and Engineering (ICBI) at the Autonomous University of Hidalgo State (UAEH). He works at the 5-CITIS Cubicle, Ciudad del Conocimiento.

Dr. Villafuerte is a member of the National System of Researchers (SNI Level 2). His research focuses on nonlinear systems, time-delay systems, and distributed intelligent systems.
